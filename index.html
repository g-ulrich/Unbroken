What does it mean to be unbroken?


The word unbroken is the effect and cure of the brokenness the world is in bondage to. A Christian is unbroken because of the gospel. The gospel is the good news that Jesus Christ, the Son of God, died for our sins and rose again, eternally triumphant over the world's lawlessness, so that there is now no eternal brokenness for those who believe in Him.


Hold on, are'nt I a good person? I'm not lawless.

Your right! However, the mistake all of us do is appeal to our own standard of goodness. You see, our standard will always be higher than our friend's standards and vice versa. This means that our moral standards are shaky if they are not based off of a higher standrd.

I need you to imagine something. While reading this, picture a man in court pleading not-guilty for rape and pedophilia. The judge is given clear evidence and sentences the man to prison.

The man is considered lawless and is convicted rightly for the crime he committed even though the man pleaded not guilty. As you can see the judge appealed to the standard of the law, designed and constructed by a higher authority. The government.



THE TEST (with excerpts)

How do you stand up against God's standards? The ten Commandments:

  1. Do you have another god(s) besides the true living God?
  2. Do you worship idols?
  3. Have you used God's name in vain?
  4. Do you keep the sabbath day holy?
  5. Do you fail to honor your father and mother?
  6. Have you murdered?
  7. Have you committed adultry?
  8. Have you stolen anything?
  9. Have you spoken falsely in any matter?
  10. Have you coveted anything?

If you have answered "yes" to any of God's laws than by His perfect standard you are considered lawless and by no means good. You are broken.
