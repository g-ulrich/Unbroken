What does it mean to be unbroken?


The word unbroken is the effect and cure of the brokenness the world is in bondage to. A Christian is unbroken because of the gospel. The gospel is the good news that Jesus Christ, the Son of God, died for our sins and rose again, eternally triumphant over his enemies, so that there is now no condemnation for those who believe, but only everlasting joy.
